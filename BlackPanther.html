Black Panther

Did You Know:

♦ Black Panther is the first Black superhero in mainstream comics.

♦ Black Panther's real name is T'Challa.

♦ He is the leader of the Wakanda Tribe.

♦ Black Panther's sister, Shuri, was once the Black Panther.

♦ His physical strength comes from the Heart-Shaped Herb of Wakanda.
